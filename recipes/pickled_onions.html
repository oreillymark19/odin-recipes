Pickled Onions

Pickled red onions in a mason jar.

Ingredients

  • Red onion
  • White distilled vinegar
  • Water
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Slice onion into thin strips and place in mason jar
  2. Combine vinegar, water, and kosher salt in small pot
  3. Bring combined ingredients in pot to a boil using high heat on stovetop
  4. Immediately remove pot from heat and pour into mason jar containing onions
  5. Leave at room temperature until the mixture cools to room temperature (approx. 3 hours)
